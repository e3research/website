Welcome

E3 Research explores the intersection of environmental economics, energy systems, and ecological sustainability. The aim is to develop clear analysis and commentary on the structural challenges facing modern economies in a resource-constrained world.

Research themes include limits to growth, renewable energy systems, environmental valuation, and long-term sustainability of economic systems.
